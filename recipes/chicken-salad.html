Odin Chicken Salad Recipe


Description:

This chicken salad recipe is the best and a family favorite! I like to use leftover roast chicken or baked chicken breasts that have been sprinkled with basil or rosemary.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup blanched silvered almonds
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 cups chopped, cooked chicken meat
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped

Steps:

  1. Toast almonds in a frying pan over medium-high heat, shaking freuently.
  2. Mix together mayonnaise, lemon juice and pepper in a medium bowl.
  3. Toss with chicken, toasted almonds and celery.
  4. Place on a sandwhich, wrap in your favorite tortilla shell or eat in a bowl! Enjoy!
