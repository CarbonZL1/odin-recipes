Odin Lasagna Recipe

Description:

This is a homemade lasagna recipe with a meaty, made-from-scratch tomato sauce and a deliciously cheesy filling. It is more traditional and will surely impress everyone you create it for.

Ingredients:

  • Meats: A pound of ground meat (1/2 pound pork, 1/2 pound lean ground beef)
  • Onions: A diced onion is cooked until transluscent with the ground meat
  • Canned Tomatoes: A can of tomato sauce and a can of crushed tomatoes.
  • Fresh Herbs: For fresh flavor, chop two tablespoons of parsley and crush one clove of garlic.
  • Sugar: A dash of sugar balances out all of the acidity from the tomatoes.
  • Spices and Seasonings: Season with dried basil, dried oregano, salt and black pepper.
  • Noodles: Uncooked noodles are best, but you can use oven-ready variety to save time.
  • Cheese: Use cottage cheese and parmasean for the layer. Add shredded mozzarella for the topping.
  • Eggs: Eggs make the cheese layer extra creamy. Plus, they act as a binding agent to hold it all together.

Steps:

  1. Cook the meat: Cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly. Add the onions and continue cooking until it's transluscent. Stir in the canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano and sugar.
  2. Cook the noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they're al dente.
  3. Make the cheese layer: Mix cottage cheese, Parmasean cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Bake the lasagna: Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven at 375 degrees F for about half an hour. Remove the foil and continue baking until the top is golden brown.
