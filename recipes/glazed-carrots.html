Odin Glazed Carrots Recipe


Description:

Glazed carrot sticks coated in a mixture of brown sugar and butter are a versatile, easy side dish for your Thanksgiving dinner, Sunday night ham or weeknight meatloaf.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds carrots, peeled and cut into sticks
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground white pepper

Steps:

  1. Place carrots into a large saucepan, pour enough water to reach depth of 1 inch, bring to boil.
  2. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer carrots until tender, 8-10 minutes. Drain and transfer to a bowl.
  3. Melt butter in same saucepan over low heat. Stir in brown sugar, salt and white pepper until dissolved.
  4. Add carrots to the saucepan and toss until coated in the brown sugar mixture. Cook and stir carrots until heated thoroughly, 1-3 minutes.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
